Mobile phone data to fight epidemics

Previous Next

Claro social project in partnership with UNICEF to develop diseases prevention study through human behavior, using mobile data.

CDR data from the data lake were used in order to understand the mobility pattern of anonymized customers, through scikit-mobility, and consequently generate actions such as sanitary barriers in cities with a large influx of infected people, especially zika virus, a tropical disease.
