Presentation of the study Where to focus sales force? at the Spatial Data Science in Financial Services Summit 2021.

Considering that Brazil has a large territory, this study aimed to allocate the geographic distribution of the sales force, that is, prioritizing the positioning of field salespeople. Only public data available were used to create sales leads, commercial establishments that are able to be registered by a sales executive and consequently run PicPay products. From this list was necessary to understand the geographic distribution of around 9 million leads and geocode them. So, instead of using some kind of paid API, it was decided to create an own geocoder by using databases from IBGE.

The next step was a territory clustering based on k-means and max-p classifiers. Subsequently the data were made available on a CARTO dashboard so that sales executives could have mobile access to this content during their door to door daily tasks. Regarding understanding some patterns in different areas and comparing them, some transactional data have been collected in order to evaluate key performance indicators.

The steps developed cover the following tools and languages: AWS Athena, Databricks, CARTO, SQL, Python, PySpark.
